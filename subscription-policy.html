Key points: Subscriptions renew automatically. You can cancel at any time from your account. Refunds are available within 7 days of registration. Your access continues until the end of your paid period after cancellation.

1. Subscription

The Service is offered on a subscription basis for a fee ("Subscription"). The subscription renews automatically until you cancel. By purchasing a trial or a Subscription, you agree that you will be charged the applicable Subscription Fee at the beginning of your subscription period and on each renewal date thereafter until you cancel, at the then-current Subscription Fee.

The subscription period is either one month (a 28-day period) or one year (a 365-day period).

All prices applicable to you are stated on the payment page; please always read the payment page carefully. We may charge any of the fees in a single transaction or in a number of separate transactions.

2. Payment method

Payment will be charged to the payment method you provided at the time of purchase at the payment page (after entering your payment method details). You authorise us (or our third-party payment processor) to automatically charge the applicable Subscription Fees on each renewal date to the payment method that you provided until you cancel your Subscription.

You authorise PDFtoLedger to supply your payment details to a third-party payment provider for processing your payments. Your credit/debit card provider may charge you currency conversion fees and other charges for processing your payments.

3. Paid trial

We may offer a paid trial for the Service for a small initial payment.

If you do not cancel your trial during the 7-day trial period, you will be automatically charged the full Subscription Fee indicated on the payment page at the end of the 7-day trial period.

We may also from time to time offer discounted offers that automatically renew at the full, non-discounted price after the end of the discount period.

4. Cancellation

Your subscription renews automatically at the end of each subscription period until you cancel. To avoid being charged for the next period, cancel your subscription before the end of the then-current period.

You can cancel your Subscription in any of the following ways:

  • By logging into your account and clicking My account → Membership → Cancel subscription
  • By contacting our customer support team via email at support@pdftoledger.com

You will be responsible for all Subscription Fees (plus any applicable taxes and other charges) incurred for the then-current Subscription period. If you cancel, your right to use the Service will continue until the end of your then-current subscription period and will then terminate without further charges.

We may cancel your Subscription if you fail to pay for your Subscription, violate our Terms of Use, or for any other reason at our sole discretion.

If you are a customer located in Colombia, you have the right to request a refund in the following cases:

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@pdftoledger.com within five (5) business days from the date you became aware of the issue. Once your request is verified, a full refund for the affected transaction will be issued to the original payment method.
